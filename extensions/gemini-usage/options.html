Gemini Usage Tracker

Reads your Gemini usage gauges (current 5-hour window and weekly) from gemini.google.com/usage every ~10 minutes and reports them to your telemetry platform. Register a usage-type resource in the dashboard and paste its key here.

Keep a gemini.google.com tab open. Google renders the usage figures client-side, so unlike the Claude tracker this needs a live tab to read them from. You must also be logged in to Gemini in this browser.
